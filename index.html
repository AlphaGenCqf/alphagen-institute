AlphaGen Institute for Cognitive & Quantitative Finance Research

Independent Research Institute

The AlphaGen Institute is an independent research body focused on the study of financial markets through cognitive, quantitative, and regime-based frameworks. Our research emphasizes diagnostic modeling, adaptive market stability, and the structural limits of prediction in non-stationary financial systems.

Research Focus

  • Belief–Reality Divergence and regime diagnostics
  • Adaptive and cognitive market frameworks
  • Volatility, stability, and structural risk analysis

Selected Research Outputs

  • Belief–Reality Divergence and Market Regime Stability (SSRN)
  • Evolutionary Market Intelligence Framework for Self-Adaptive Quantitative Systems (SSRN)
  • AlphaGen: An Adaptive Algorithmic Trading Ecosystem (SSRN)
