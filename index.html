AlphaGen Institute for Cognitive & Quantitative Finance Research

Independent Research Institute

The AlphaGen Institute is an independent research body focused on understanding financial markets through cognitive, quantitative, and regime-based frameworks. Our research emphasizes diagnostic modeling, adaptive market stability, and the structural limits of prediction in non-stationary financial systems.

Research Focus

  • Belief–Reality Divergence and regime diagnostics
  • Adaptive and cognitive market frameworks
  • Volatility, stability, and structural risk analysis
