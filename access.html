Research Access & Collaboration

AlphaGen research is developed as a diagnostic-first, audit-grade framework for regime-aware quantitative systems. Public papers and artifacts establish methodological clarity, reproducibility, and theoretical grounding.

Deeper evaluation, system-level discussion, and deployment-oriented access are intentionally gated.

What Is Public

  • Working papers and conference research outputs
  • Conceptual frameworks and diagnostic definitions
  • Selected reproducible experimental artifacts
  • High-level system architecture and validation logic

What Is Restricted

  • Full execution and capital-scaled risk pipelines
  • Live regime-transition handling mechanisms
  • Long-horizon coherence enforcement internals (Eterna)
  • Institutional stress-testing and evaluation tooling

Intended Collaborations

This research is designed for engagement with:

  • Quantitative hedge funds and proprietary trading desks
  • Institutional research groups evaluating regime-aware systems
  • Strategy research teams seeking diagnostic overlays rather than signal alpha

This work is not intended for retail deployment, signal vending, or black-box licensing.

Engagement Model

  1. Independent review of public research
  2. Contextual discussion of regime and diagnostic assumptions
  3. Scoped evaluation access where appropriate
  4. Advisory or collaborative alignment if mutual fit exists
